Four Palestinians were killed along the fence east of Al-Bureij in the center of the Gaza Strip, while one died east of Gaza City, al-Qidra said, according to al-Jazeera. The six victims were identified as: Ahmed Ibrahim Zaki al-Taweel (27), Mohammed Abdulhafid Youssef Ismail (29), Ahmed Ahmed Abdallah Abu Naeem (17), Abdallah Barham Ibrahim Suleiman al-Daghma (25) and Afifi Mahmoud Atta Afifi (18) and Tamer Iyad Mahmoud Abu Armanah.
